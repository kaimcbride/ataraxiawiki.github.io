Wemos d1 Mini⌗

Useful facts⌗

Powering from 5V⌗

This voltage regulator is capable of supply 500mA. As mentioned in the technical specification, the Wemos D1 Mini consumes around 71mA of current when transmitting, although it is capable of consuming up to 500mA peak current.
